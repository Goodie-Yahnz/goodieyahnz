Anim A Kri: Free Web based CSS animation app by Goodie Yahnz

Anim A Kri lets you create animations right in your browser. No downloads, no installations, no logging in, no signing, just open, animate and record!

Anim A Kri or Kri is the second app of Goodie Yahnz. It was created on September 11th but launched on 26th December 2024 in Abia, Nigeria by Goodiey Cheaziey.

Anim A Kri is used to animate, simply by pasting different image URLs in a code thereby joining the images together to create a frame by frame animation.
